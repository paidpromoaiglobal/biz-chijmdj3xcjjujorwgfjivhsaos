About Us

Breathe and Shine Academy

Breathe and Shine Academy is your wellness sanctuary in the heart of Ayappakkam, Chennai. We are dedicated to helping you discover the transformative power of yoga, meditation, and holistic fitness — designed for all ages and fitness levels.

Our experienced instructors guide you through authentic practices that strengthen your body, calm your mind, and elevate your spirit. Whether you are a complete beginner or an advanced practitioner, we create a warm and supportive space where you can grow at your own pace.

  • Expert-led yoga and meditation sessions for all levels
  • Holistic wellness programs for mind, body, and soul
  • Warm, welcoming space in Ayappakkam, Chennai
